RSA

 - Unlike systemmetric algorithms like AES, RSA uses a mathematically linked key pait, anything encrypted
    with the public key can only be decrypted with the private key, vice versa
 - Asymmetry solves the key distribution problem that plagued symmetric encryption
 - Freely share public key with the entire world without compromised secruity
